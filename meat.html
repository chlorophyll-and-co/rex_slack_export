make an H shaped galvanized pipe base, with a pipe that comes out about 4 ft. One pipe on a t comes up from the middle and then has two nipples attached to it, about 8" apart. The pipes are attached to the pallet with cable ties.

2017-08-03 16:49
I put a quick connect and a shut off valve on the end of the pipe that sticks out, so that i can disconnect and then drag it to the next paddock.

2017-08-03 16:53
this is the third iteration of the system, and has the following benefits over other systems I?ve seen: - pallet prevents pigs from spilling extra water and making a wallow - can be quickly moved from one paddock ot the next by one person - nipples cannot be ripped out (unlike systems that are attached directly to a barrel) - can be attached to a barrel or a hose (using low pressure nipples)
